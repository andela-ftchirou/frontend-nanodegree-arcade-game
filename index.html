Can you reach the next rive ?

Be aware of the creepy bugs on the road. Clear as many levels as you can.

Controls

  • ↑ Move the player up.
  • ↓ Move the player down.
  • → Move the player/selector to the right.
  • ← Move the player/selector to the left.
  • h Redeem 1 life against an item.
  • p Pause/Resume the game.
  • q Quit and show the player selection screen.

Items

  • Gives you one more life.
  • Makes you indestructible for 1 second.
  • Unlocks immediately the next level.
  • Turns water in stone for 1 second.
  • Slows down the bugs for 1 second.
  • Freezes the bugs for 1 second.
